Early Blight

Early Blight is one of common and the most important anamorphic fungal disease in the potato growing regions. A potato grower has to follow effective control measures against the early blight of potato to avoid severe loss in total crop production. Early blight of potato is a very common disease in India, the other important disease of potato is late blight of India. Identify the symptoms to control or manage it. Although, the intensity of latter is comparatively less. It can cause up to 40% loss in yield of tubers. Damage is more severe when attack occurs during the time of tuber formation. The disease appears at the early or young stage of plant. It is a disease of cool as well as warm regions. Hence, it is common in hills as well as in the plains.
